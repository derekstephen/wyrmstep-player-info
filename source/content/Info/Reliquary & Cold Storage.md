# Reliquary & Cold Storage

---

## What the Reliquary Is

**Artifacts retrieved from Doors do not pass directly into use. They are held in the Reliquary first.**

Planar items are unstable. They carry resonance signatures from whatever Door they came through, and that resonance needs to be processed before the item is safe to use in the field — or even to handle freely. The Reliquary is where that processing happens. Silas oversees it. Nafas is aware of everything stored there.

What an artifact is, what it can do, and what it costs to use — none of that becomes clear until processing is complete. You will not know the item's full properties until Silas tells you.

---

## How Processing Works

When an artifact enters Cold Storage at the end of a session, standard processing takes **one full rotation** before the artifact is unlocked and moved to the Guild Vault.

During that rotation, Silas posts updates in **#reliquary** as the process develops. Do not attempt to access or use an item still listed as In Processing.

---

## Accelerating Processing

Bench players can dedicate Garrison Task slots to speed up artifact processing. Future tasks are available top achieve this as the Guild Rank advances.

---

## When Processing Is Complete

Silas posts the artifact's full properties in **#reliquary**. The item then moves to the Guild Vault and enters the Iron Ledger — at which point it is available for checkout through the normal item request process.

Everything the item can do is revealed at this stage. Not before.

---

## Fading NPCs

Sometimes the people you encounter inside Doors are falling apart — planar instability bleeding into whoever or whatever has been inside too long. You will be able to tell if you pay attention. The signs are there.

**If you encounter a Fading NPC, report it to Silas immediately.** Do not wait. Do not assume there is time. Fading is not reversible once it has progressed past a certain point, and what can be done depends entirely on how quickly the Bastion acts. Silas will advise on next steps.

---

## The Reliquary and the Iron Ledger

Once an artifact clears Cold Storage and enters the Guild Vault, it is subject to the same rules as every other guild item — it belongs to the guild, it can be checked out, and it can potentially be bought out at 150% market value. An artifact that is still In Processing cannot be checked out, cannot be claimed, and cannot be traded or transferred.
