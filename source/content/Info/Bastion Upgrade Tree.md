# Bastion Upgrade Tree

---

## How Upgrades Work

**The Bastion grows with the guild. Fund an upgrade, and Silas builds it. Nothing is instant.**

Upgrades are proposed and funded through the **Guild Wallet** — the communal fund fed by the Tithe. Once a project is approved and fully funded, Silas begins construction. Every upgrade takes **two sessions to complete** from the moment funding is confirmed. Plan around that timeline.

There are no refunds. Committed gold is committed. Think before you propose.

---

## How to Propose an Upgrade

Post your upgrade proposal in **downtime-rp**. Include the upgrade name and confirm you are ready to fund it. Silas will confirm the cost and any prerequisites, and will state whether the upgrade is currently buildable.

Full payment is required upfront. Once Silas confirms, the two-session build clock begins.

---

## Tier 1 — Available Now

These upgrades are available from campaign start and require no prerequisites beyond the funding cost.

---

### Mess Hall — 250gp

The Bastion's common area gets a proper kitchen and supply rotation. Bench players and returning active teams have access to free rations between sessions, and the navigation support infrastructure improves — Keys entering the field are better fed and better oriented.

**Effect:** Free rations between sessions. Improved outcomes on Navigation Support Garrison tasks.

---

### Echo-Chamber Tuning — 350gp

The resonance chambers that allow Silas to maintain contact with Keys inside Doors are recalibrated and strengthened. The connection between the Bastion and the active team becomes cleaner and more stable.

**Effect:** Stronger Anchor connection. Improved Memory Anchor reliability. Reduces risk of communication loss during sessions.

---

### Whispering Library — 400gp

A dedicated research and indexing space for the growing body of planar knowledge the Wyrmsteppers accumulate. Materials gathered through Garrison Duty and missions are catalogued and made cross-referenceable.

**Effect:** Improved outcomes on Planar Research Garrison tasks. Accumulated intelligence persists and compounds across rotations rather than being isolated to individual sessions.

---

### Key-Master's Toolbench — 450gp

A proper workstation for handling, examining, and enhancing the unusual objects Keys recover from Doors. Safer handling procedures for unstable curios. Minor enhancement capabilities for soulbound items.

**Effect:** Safer handling of retrieved artifacts. Unlocks minor enhancement options for soulbound items. Reduces risk on Forge & Lab crafting tasks.

---

## Tier 2 — Unlocks at Journeyman Rank

Tier 2 upgrades become available when the guild reaches Journeyman rank. Each Tier 2 project requires at least one specific Tier 1 upgrade as a prerequisite — you cannot build on a foundation that does not exist.

Costs scale up significantly at this tier. Begin building your Guild Wallet before you need it, not after.

The full list of Tier 2 options and their prerequisites will be shared by Silas when the guild reaches Journeyman.

---

## Tier 3 — Unlocks at Vanguard Rank

Tier 3 upgrades require both Vanguard rank and specific materials that cannot be purchased — they must be retrieved from designated Doors. Gold alone will not build these.

Ask Silas which Doors carry the materials required for a project you have in mind. That intelligence exists. You may need to plan a mission around acquiring it.

---

## Planning Your Build Order

A few things to consider when thinking about Bastion investment:

The **Guild Vault overflow rule** applies here — gold above the Iron Coffer cap automatically converts to Construction Points. If your Vault is frequently capped, you are already passively generating build resources. Track the Wallet closely in **📊┃structural-reports**.

Garrison Duty Task 05 (Garrison Recruitment) directly supports construction projects through successful rolls. Bench players who prioritize this task contribute to the build queue even without direct gold investment.

Upgrades are permanent. They do not degrade, do not require maintenance, and do not expire. Invest in what the guild actually needs, not just what sounds useful.
